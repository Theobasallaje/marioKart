Track Highlights

Rainbow Roads is famous for its long, twisting path through the sky and dynamic music. On this page, you'll find tips for staying on track and mastering the boost pads.

Why it works

The combination of high-speed straights and floating lanes rewards precision, so characters with good handling are strong here.
